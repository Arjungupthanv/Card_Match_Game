## ğŸ¯ Game Rules

1. Click on any card to flip it and reveal a Greek letter
2. Click on a second card to find its matching pair
3. If the cards match, they stay face-up
4. If they don't match, you lose 4 points and both cards flip back
5. Match all 12 pairs to win!
